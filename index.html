The rules are as follows:
  1. Only MECH domains are encouraged.
  2. Maximum of 3 authors and only student authors allowed. It is not a necessity that all the authors must be from the same institute.
  3. We take plagiarism very seriously. Only original research works are accepted.
  4. The paper must not have been presented or published anywhere else.
  5. The paper must be in two column IEEE format. This format must be strictly adhered to. The paper length must be limited to 6 pages.
  6. The paper must be in .pdf or .doc or .docx formats.
  7. The paper must be sent through mail. The instructions are as follows:
    1. The subject must be the title of the paper.
    2. The body of the mail should include - Name and Institute of participant (starting from the first author and going till the third one), Department and Year, Contact numbers, Email addresses.
    3. Send the paper as an attachment with this mail.
    4. The email id is : throttlez2k21@gmail.com
